Tic-Tac-Toe Cypher

Explore the 34 unique patterns derived from a tic-tac-toe game grid.

Use the dropdown filter to refine your view by specific characteristics or tags.

Characteristics are defined by: s/ tags for symmetry; r/ tags define the categories of r/logic, r/direction, and r/counting; t/pairwise indicates the 22 patterns that are of a pairwise reflection type.
